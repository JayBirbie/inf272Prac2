Biography and Background

Jessica Turner, from Cape Town, Western Cape, is enrolled in a Bachelor of Information Science program. She has always been drawn to data and its potential to solve complex problems, particularly in industries like healthcare and finance. Jessica is passionate about using data analytics to improve public health systems in South Africa, with the aim of making healthcare more accessible and efficient for everyone.
