Biography and Background

Jay Mashele, a Johannesburg native, is studying for a Bachelor of Information Technology with a focus on Software Development. A natural problem-solver, Jay loves building things from scratch and is passionate about creating innovative tech solutions. She is particularly interested in developing software for South African startups and has a dream of designing a locally inspired video game that showcases the rich culture and stories of her homeland.
