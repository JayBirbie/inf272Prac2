Biography and Background

Ryan van Wyk comes from Pretoria, Gauteng, and is pursuing a Bachelor of Commerce in Informatics. Ryan is fascinated by how technology can optimize business processes and drive efficiency. He chose this degree to bridge the gap between technology and business, with the goal of helping companies streamline their operations through digital transformation. Ryan aspires to work in tech consulting and lead projects that revolutionize industries across Africa.
