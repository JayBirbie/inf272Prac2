Biography and Background

Raelene Dookkoo is from Durban, KwaZulu-Natal, and is pursuing a Bachelor of Commerce in Informatics. She chose this degree because it combines her love for technology with her interest in business applications. Raelene is particularly passionate about using data-driven insights to improve decision-making in South African industries. Her goal is to develop innovative tech solutions that address challenges in education and empower underserved communities through accessible digital tools.

Hobbies

Raelene has a deep love for the outdoors and enjoys spending her free time hiking along the Drakensberg Mountains. She’s also an avid photographer and runs a small travel blog where she shares photos of South Africa’s landscapes. When she’s not exploring nature, Raelene likes experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen.
