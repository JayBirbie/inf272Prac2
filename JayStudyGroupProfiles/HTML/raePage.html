Biography and Background

Raelene Dookkoo is from Durban, KwaZulu-Natal, and is pursuing a Bachelor of Commerce in Informatics. She chose this degree because it combines her love for technology with her interest in business applications. Raelene is passionate about using data-driven insights to improve decision-making in South African industries.

Hobbies

Raelene enjoys hiking along the Drakensberg Mountains, photography, and runs a travel blog showcasing South Africa's natural beauty. She also enjoys experimenting with new recipes.
